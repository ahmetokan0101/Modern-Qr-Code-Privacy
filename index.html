Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Modern QR Code is a mobile application that allows users to scan, create, and share QR codes. This Privacy Policy explains how our app protects user privacy and how personal data is collected, used, and shared.

2. Data Collection

Our app may collect the following data:

  • Camera access (for QR code scanning)
  • Storage access (for saving and sharing QR codes)
  • WiFi network information (for connecting to WiFi networks via QR codes)
  • App usage data (for debugging and improvement purposes)

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • QR code scanning and generation functionality
  • WiFi network connection
  • Opening and sharing URLs
  • App performance improvement
  • Debugging and troubleshooting

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Google ML Kit (for QR code scanning)
  • Advertising services (for in-app ads)
  • URL opening services
  • Legal requirements

5. Data Security

Your data security is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

6. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Right to manage app permissions
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to refuse using the app

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes.
